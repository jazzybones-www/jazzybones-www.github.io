# i found my phone

a couple of days ago i went for a swim. when i got back home and to my room, i
realized that i didn't have my phone.

turns out, i put it on top of the dryer as i put my wet clothes into the washing
machine. it took me until today to find it for three reasons:

1. i'm not addicted to my phone, so i wasn't looking very hard
1. i'm too lazy to actually get up and search for it for an extended period of
   time
1. i'm really bad at finding things

this kind of reminded me of a story from when i was younger. for a few years
when i was in elementary school, every easter my mom would hide around $50 in
ones and fives for me and my siblings to find. i've always been really bad at
searching for things, so i'd try and get out of it. she'd force me to play, and
predictably, i'd be very bad at the game. then my mom would feel bad and give me
hints.

those hints were probably the worst part of the entire thing. it felt like i was
being shamed in front of my entire family, and getting paid so that i couldn't
complain. like, i didn't want to play the game, so i shouldn't have had to play
the game.
