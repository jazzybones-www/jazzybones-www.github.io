# my relationship with sleep

my sleep schedule is constantly all over the place. sometimes i'll wake up at 4
pm, sometimes i'll go to sleep at 4 pm. sometimes i get 12 hours of sleep,
sometimes i get 0. it's just this giant mess that usually averages around 8
hours per day, but not really.

this makes me lose track of time. when my sleep schedule is bad, sometimes i
almost forget to brush my teeth because it's just not dark. when i don't have
ceremonies at the beginning or end of each day, days stop becoming relevant.
time just passes, and occasionally i sleep.

throughout the "day", i usually don't feel very tired. i get irritable really
fast, i can lose focus on things quickly, and i get tired quickly if i do
anything too strenuous, but i don't feel tired specifically. it's more that i
just observe the way that i react to things and understand "i am tired right
now". it's the same way with hunger. occasionally i just go "oops, looks like
i'm shaking again!" and eat.

whenever i am tired, it ruins my mental health. seventh grade, the worst year of
my life, was bad for many reasons, but one of the biggest ones was that i just
didn't get enough sleep. i had an early math class and stayed up late doing
homework, which made me irritable. it also made me procrastinate my homework for
the night, perpetuating the cycle.

sometimes i wish that i could just stay up forever without sleeping, but then i
realize that that would suck. we can't just be productive 24/7, we need some
downtime, and without sleep, i think we'd lose a lot of that downtime.
