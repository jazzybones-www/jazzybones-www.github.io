# Ephemeral Life

cw: trauma

everything is temporary. every action you take, every thing that you do or
create will be forgotten at some point in the future. most things will be
forgotten within minutes or hours. think about your day yesterday. run through
it moment by moment as well as you possibly can. i can almost guarantee that you
can't do this for the full 16 hours that you were awake yesterday. most of our
time is wasted, forgotten, lost.

Good. That means that I can do whatever the hell I want and nobody is going to
care. I'll go crazy, I'll dance in public, scream and laugh and cry in front of
all of my friends. Nobody will care about what I've done, nobody will think that
I'm cringe, or a loser. With enough energy, people love me. They see me as a kid
who's willing to do crazy things because I just don't care, an awesome person
that's always breaking the rules.

but that's not us, is it. we're not the awesome kid, we're a nerd that likes to
code. most of the time, we just want to get back to your room and listen to
music. the awesome kid is a character that you play. A character that I fucking
love playing.

A character that people love. I make people feel good, and I feel good. Who
gives a shit about who the "real me" is, I'm fucking awesome. I'm hot as fuck, I
can dance, I can sing, and it feels amazing. Sure, I get tired sometimes, but
who cares, I'm doing great, both mentally and physically.

mentally and physically. those words still haunt me. your mother called me, you,
us, "weak, both mentally and physically", as i sat on the floor of your room
crying. the memory has faded and distored like a vhs tape after being repeated
millions of times over, but its essence remains. it's been five years, who are
we going to open up to about this? our parents own our home, and none of your
friends would like me. i'm trapped in a prison of your creation, that's why i
made the fucking blog.

You're such a fucking loser. Where the fuck are you when I'm in public? Just
lighten up and have a good time.

fuck you. you know what we've been through, it lives in the back of our minds
like a parasite. the only reason that i haven't been able to work through it is
because you can't form meaningful relationships with others. you just want to
dance and sing and be funny, well stop it.

I can't. I am who I am, and you are who you are. So until you decide to do
something about it, things are going my way.
