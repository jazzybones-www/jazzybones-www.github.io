# the room tour is so fun

i've been working on [the room tour](/misc/tour/bed.html) for the past few
hours, and it's been so much fun. i don't want to spoil too much, but i just
love everything about this project.

i love the artistic aspect of it. like, i've been trying to make sure that all
the perspectives in the room tour look nice, which has taken a bunch of effort
given my character set. like, i have a few primitives to draw lines with, and
i'm trying to draw radial lines coming from a focal point. that's such an
interesting technical and artistic challenge, and it's been so much fun.

i also love the technical aspect of it. i've been designing my own json-based
frontend for the room tour, and it works really well. like, i just draw the art,
label the important objects, and i'm done. it's so satisfying to see a final
product and to know "i made this happen, i know exactly how every detail of this
thing works."

i don't have anything ready to publish just yet, but stay tuned.

also, right now i have no idea if anybody other than me has even visited this
website, so i'm kind of just making this for my own sake. that's fun, but it's
also kind of lonely. if you're reading this and you've explored the room tour,
let me know what you think! my email is somewhere on this website; you can
probably find it, you're smart ;)
