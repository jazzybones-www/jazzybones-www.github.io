# the jazzybones word: tear

when i say "tear", i could be referring to a torn shirt, or a tear in your eyes,
or a "tier" list, or maybe i'm "taring" a scale. webster's online dictionary has
a few more definitions for each of these words, including "tier", as in a person
who ties things, which is pronounced like the word "tire", which could refer to
a wheel or to exhaustion.

if you're a native english speaker, then you've almost certainly already know
all of these definitions, but somehow whenever we hear the word "tear" we can
just intuitively figure it out through context.

i think it's kind of neat that our brains are able to just do that in real time
without us even noticing. humans are so cool.
