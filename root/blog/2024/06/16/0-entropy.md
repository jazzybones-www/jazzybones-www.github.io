# the jazzybones word: entropy

you know when you have earbuds in your pocket and you take them out to listen to
music but the wires get all tangled? there's a mathematical reason for that.

imagine a robot taking a random walk around a field. every second, it can either
go straight, turn left, or turn right. if i let this robot run around for a few
minutes, there is an astronomically small possibility that this robot just picks
"go straight" every time and walks in a perfect line, but that will almost never
happen. in the majority of cases, the robot will turn around, double back, and
cross over its own path over and over again in this tangled, chaotic mess.

a similar thing happens to your earbuds. the probability that your earbuds
jumble around into a perfectly untangled coil is just far less likely than the
probability that your earbuds jumble around into a big knot.

the general level of chaos within a system is called "entropy", and it is a
fundamental property of the universe that entropy will always increase. the
order of a hot cake and cold ice cream will collapse into a chaotic
room-temperature soup, the beauty of life will always end, and even the
brightest galaxies will fizzle out into nothingness eventually.

yet somehow, we make art. we write, we sing, we dance, we play, and we build.
right now, you are reading words. these words are order manifested in the usual
chaos of my ssd. this is what humanity does, we create beautiful order out of a
beautiful world. we have a limited amount of entropy to use in the world, let's
try and use it well.
